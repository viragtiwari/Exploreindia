Howrah Brigde

Howrah Bridge, an iconic symbol of Kolkata, spans the Hooghly River, connecting the city with the Howrah district. Completed in 1943, it is a marvel of engineering, with no nuts or bolts used in its construction. The bridge's majestic presence and intricate design attract visitors and stand as a testament to Kolkata's historical charm.
